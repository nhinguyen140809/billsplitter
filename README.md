# 💰 BILL SPLITTER

*Fair & simple expense sharing for groups!*

## 📌 Introduction
Bill Splitter is a web application designed to help users track and manage debts within a group. The app records who owes whom and how much, allowing members to keep a clear overview of their financial relationships. It can be used in many situations such as sharing expenses for meals, trips, or household costs, but also more generally for keeping track of any money borrowed or owed between members. By organizing these records and optimizing transactions, the app makes it easier for groups to settle their debts efficiently.

## 🚀 Features
✔️ **Flexible Bill Splitting** – Split expenses equally or assign custom amounts to each participant.

✔️ **Smart Transaction Optimization** – Automatically reduces the number of payments needed to settle debts.

✔️ **Easy Expense Tracking** – Add and manage multiple expenses with a clear breakdown for each member.

✔️ **User-Friendly Interface** – Simple and intuitive design for quick and hassle-free use.

✔️ **Local Data Storage** – All data is stored locally in the user’s browser.

✔️ **Multiple Settlements Management** – Create and manage multiple settlements for different groups or occasions.

## 📖 How To Use
1. **Add Participants** – Enter names of people sharing the bill.
1. **Enter Expenses** – Add the total amount and select either:
    - *Equal Split*: Automatically divides the bill equally among all participants.
    - *Unequal Split*: Manually assign specific amounts to each person.

1. **View Results** – The system calculates the most efficient way to settle payments.

1. **Make Payments** – Pay each other based on the calculated transactions.
